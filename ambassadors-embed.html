she/her

I’m a Machine Learning Engineer at Arm, focused on accelerating ML performance, benchmarking, and deployment on Arm CPUs through projects like KleidiAI and ExecuTorch—helping make PyTorch faster and more efficient on billions of devices. Before this, I built robotic perception systems at Dyson, deploying multi-task vision models on embedded hardware. As a PyTorch Ambassador, I’m passionate about open source and committed to making AI more accessible through real-world PyTorch applications, mentorship, and community initiatives.
